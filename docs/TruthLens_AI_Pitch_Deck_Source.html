TruthLens AISlide 1
The Problem

Remote interviews are harder to trust

Recruiters depend on human judgement

Confidence, stress, or suspicious behavior are often inferred manually and inconsistently.

No single system fuses all signals

Most platforms stop at video calling and do not combine face, voice, movement, and scene intelligence.

Behavioral red flags are missed in real time

Looking away, phone use, multiple people in frame, or stress spikes are difficult to track live.

Little evidence remains after the interview

Recruiters need structured summaries to support better decisions and reduce bias.
